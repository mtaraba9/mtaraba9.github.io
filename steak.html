Sous Vide Steak

Note: this recipe requires a sous vide (find one here)

Ingredients

  • 2 8oz filet steaks
  • 2 tablespoons of garlic flavored extra virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 sealable plastic bags

Instructions

  1. Fill large pot with water. Affix sous vide onto the side of the pot and set temperature based on chart below:
    2. Desired cook of steak Temperature range
    Rare 120F to 128F (49C to 53C)
    Medium-rare 129F to 134F (54C to 57C)
    Medium 135F to 144F (57C to 62C)
    Medium-well 145F to 155F (63C to 68C)
    Well done 156F and up (69C and up)
  2. While the water is heating season both sides of the steak with the flavored olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Turn a frying pan (preferably cast iron) to high heat. Once hot, sear each side of each steak for 30-40 seconds. Remove steaks from heat and place each into a separate plastic bag.
  4. Remove all air from each plastic bag and seal. Place sealed pastic bag into water. Ensure steaks are submerged. If there is some air left in the bag you may need to place a heavy object like a meat tenderizer on top of the steak.
  5. Leave steaks in water for at least 45 minutes. You can't overcook them because the water is not hot enough.
  6. Heat frying pan to medium-high heat. Remove steaks from bags and return to the frying pan. Cook each side until desired crustiness is achieved. This should only take 1-2 minutes per side.
  7. Enjoy your steak!
